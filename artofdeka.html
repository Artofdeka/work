Art of deka

When it was time to creat a personal brand, I wanted it to reflects my friendly personality and passion for design, illustration, and animation.

Digital design Art direction Art of deka

Where to begin

How could I transform the idea in my head into something real? With a wealth of experiences in Fine Arts, design, illustration, and animation, I had a vast pool of inspiration to draw from. So, I simply started experimenting, doodling, drawing, and creating.

What's in a Name?

At first, I named my personal brand “Deka”, only to discover numerous variations of it already in use. Realizing the need for a unique identity, I veered away from anything resembling "Deka." However, the name held sentimental value, having been my nickname for years. I was reluctant to part with it, yet I knew a solution was necessary.

As I delved into my creative process, a memory from my university days resurfaced—one that left a lasting impression. It was the moment I stumbled upon a treasure at the library: "The Art of Finding Nemo" by Pixar Animation Studios. This book offered a rare glimpse into the making of the film. I had never encountered such a resource before. From that point on, I was hooked, eagerly seeking out similar insights into the creative process.

Finally I made the connection; put The Art of Finding Nemo together with my name and voila! All that was left was a little tweaking.

The Art of Finding Nemo + Thandeka = Art of Deka
