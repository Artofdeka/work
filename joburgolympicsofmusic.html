The 947 Joburg Olympics of Music

The world’s greatest sportsmen and women were supposed to take centre stage at the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games. 947 knew that listeners were sad it was no longer happening. To make them feel better, 947 decided to give the world’s greatest artists their very own Olympics.

Digital design Art direction 947

My role

My team was tasked with creating awareness and get Joburg amped for the world’s greatest music spectacle. I drew inspiration from other artists to help create the kind of design that had bright, electric colours, and vibrancy of the REAL Olympics.

Look and feel

In my attempt to bring the message forth - that BIG, BOLD, CHARIOT OF FIRE, OLYMPIC EPICNESS! I used three key elements: Rings, torch, and city. In this case: rings of vinyl records, Joburg cityscape with the burning torch at the top of the Ponte tower.

16 Artists. 4 rounds.
1 Olympic champion of music.

Round of 16

Sixteen artists went head to head to find Joburg's ultimate Olympic champion of music in the Round of 16. Two artists competed daily to move to the next round, the artist with the most votes won. One listener was selected a winner from all the entries.

Let the voting begin

I designed voting cards which were posted on social media, for listeners to vote for their artists and help them smash the Olympic champion of music world record.
