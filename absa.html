Enhancing the Absa Gold Cheque Account onboarding journey

This is a look at how our multi-disciplinary team went through rapid iterations to enhance the Cheque standalone digital journey by optimizing business processes and utilizing available technologies to reduce drop-offs, increase straight through processing which would result in cost reduction and improved service turnaround time,

PRIMARY GOAL

Biometric authentication

Replace current biometrics system with iiDENTIFii facial biometrics platform to increase percentage of successful facial biometrics scans from 20% - 80%.

SECONDARY GOAL

Anvanced KYC

Implement real-time document OCR in order to convert FICA non-complaint customers whilst on channel or during application process.

THIRD GOAL

Automation

Implement an achestration layer in order to achieve straight-through processing, automate product fulfilment and reduce hand-offs to the contact centre.

PART ONE

THE CHALLENGE

where we wanted to be

We worked closely with the DSP design core team, stakeholders within the business, with a goal in mind of archieving at least 80% straight-through processing, automate product fullfilment, and significantly reduce hand-offs to back office nad turnaround time.

where we were

87% of customers who've been successfuly verified are later found to be non-FICA complaint due to failed facial biometric scan, failed physical address verification or medium rick rating. These customers are then required to send documents (ID and/or proof of address), which means that these applications are handed over to the FIC centre/contact centre for product fullfilment, which defeats straight-through processing, increases turnaround time and subsequesntly negatively impacts customer experience and digital chanel adoption.

How to get there

  1. Scale biometric authentication
  2. Anvanced KYC
  3. Automation of post-ID&V fulfilment

PART TWO

THE DESIGN

Wireframes & Mockups

As the design direction was established, we began creating the structure and hierarchy of the design, as well as the aesthetic treatments.

Consolidated Light details and consent screen

We combined the Light personal details and Consent screens into one screen which helped reduce the time it takes to complete the journey. We added an additional screen where customers would capture their ID number, which allows Absa to check customer's ID to check if they are a New-to-bank customer or an exisiting customer.

Personal details

Following rapid iterations and co-designing with the core design team, we settled on a consised Personal details screen that removed clutter and focused on what the customer needed, heuristics providing customers with a cleaner look, consistency in the field naming and structure.
