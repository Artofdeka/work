Africa Connected with Standard Bank, 702 & CapeTalk

Standard Bank together with 702 and Cape Talk sent renowned broadcaster Lee Kasumba across Africa to explore the countless opportunities, endless growth potential and the infinite innovation driving development, boosting investment and changing lives across the continent. listeners were able to follow her journey on 702, CapeTalk and on social media using the hashtag #AfricaConnected.

Degital design Standard Bank

The Approach

We initiated the project with a Kick-off meeting to align everyone on the team. Following that, I conducted interviews with key stakeholders to gain deeper insights into the project objectives and business expectations. Subsequently, we focused on understanding user tasks.

Design phase

Throughout the design phase, I convened with essential team members and the product manager for an ideation session, resulting in our initial wireframe depicting the site's appearance and user journey. This ensured a collective grasp of user needs and the required design approach. Subsequently, I conducted user testing with colleagues to validate the wireframes, incorporating valuable insights to fine-tune the designs for optimal usability.

Countries page

A significant issue arose on the countries page. Upon clicking the 'more insights' buttons, users were directed off-platform to Standard Bank’s website insights page, resulting in extensive loading times. This significantly diminished the user experience, especially considering that these pages were visited primarily for this vital information.

The proposed solution involved hosting this information directly on the portal, aiming to enhance its reliability and streamline the user experience.
