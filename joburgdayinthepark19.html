Huawei Joburg Day In The Park 2019

The Huawei Joburg Day music festival autumn edition took place on Saturday 4 May 2019 and fans and families came out to experience a day in the city they love, and were treated to an entire day of live music from some of SA’s biggest multi-generational artists.

An iconic look for an iconic event

Since the line-up for the event had mix of new, current and some of SA's legendary music artists, my focus was on creating a nostalgic retro look that took advantage of a vibrant colour pallet and Joburg's tenacious vibe. To do that I drew inspiration from some of the NBA creatives that fit my narrative.

Launch Day

We launched Huawei Joburg in the Park 2019, and posted social media ecards on multiple social media platforms. By the end of the launch Joburg Day was one of the top 5 trending topics on twitter in SA.
