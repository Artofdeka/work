Huawei Joburg Day:

One stage. SA's most sought-after artists.

The annual Huawei Joburg Day music festival spring edition took place on Saturday 1 September and fans were treated to a line-up made of the biggest hitmakers, award-winners and chart-toppers in the country.

The challenge

Increase listener engagement, ticket sales, and make this year’s event the biggest Joburg Day ever.

947 wanted to have a more engaging and interactive conversation with their listeners, so as part of early bird tickets sale launch, listeners were given an opportunity to choose who they wanted to see perform.

Choose your line-up

Phase one

We came up with a campaign called "Choose Your Line-Up" for the first phase. I partnered with a back-end developer to build a platform that let listeners pick their favourite artists and share them on social media. We asked listeners to pick five artists they wanted to see perform at Joburg Day from a list 20 South African musicians, and then share their “fav’ list” with their friends.

Phase two: Official line-up

The Official launch included all collateral for the campaign, website with the official artist line-up, venue and other content.

Artist ecards and banners

I designed artist ecards and digital banners that were posted on social media. I also designed digital billboards, street poles and newspaper ads.

947 homepage takeover

The print collateral

From foam hands to foam phones

Every year we produce "Foam Hands" as part of the merchandise for the event. This year Huawei asked I design Foam "Phones" instead, as part of celebrating their Huawei P20 | P20 Pro phone, as this edition of Joburg Day was Captured by the Huawei P20 | P20 Pro phones.
